PKU Architecture & Circuit Innovations For Intelligent Chips

Tianyu Jia 贾天宇

Assistant Professor · School of Integrated Circuits, Peking University

My research focuses on exploring efficient AI hardware including domain-specific accelerators, compute-in-memory architectures, LLM-assisted agile chip design, and efficient power/thermal management.

Google Scholar

Prior to joining Peking University, I was an Assistant Research Professor at the Department of ECE, Carnegie Mellon University. I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Harvard Architecture, Circuits, and Compilers group at Harvard University (2020-2021). I obtained the Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering (Best Thesis Award) from Northwestern University in 2019. My research has been published at top-tier venues including ISSCC, JSSC, VLSI, MICRO, DAC, ISCA, ASPLOS, etc.
